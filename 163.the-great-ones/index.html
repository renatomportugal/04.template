Earl Hines

Earl Kenneth Hines, universally known as Earl "Fatha" Hines, was an American jazz pianist and bandleader.

Ella Fitzgerald

Ella Jane Fitzgerald was an American jazz singer often referred to as the First Lady of Song, Queen of Jazz and Lady Ella.

Nat King Cole

Nathaniel Adams Coles, known professionally as Nat King Cole, was an American singer who first came to prominence as a leading jazz pianist.

Oscar Peterson

Oscar Emmanuel Peterson, CC, CQ, OOnt was a Canadian jazz pianist and composer.

Billie Holiday

Eleanora Fagan, professionally known as Billie Holiday, was an American jazz musician and singer-songwriter.

Erroll Garner

Erroll Louis Garner was an American jazz pianist and composer known for his swing playing and ballads.

Dizzy Gillespie

John Birks "Dizzy" Gillespie was an American jazz trumpeter, bandleader, composer and occasional singer.

Bud Powell

Earl Rudolph "Bud" Powell was a jazz pianist, born and raised in Harlem, New York City.
